About Yedibasak

Anchored in trust, rooted in community.

Since our founding, Yedibasak has paired humanitarian relief with education, health, and community development. Local partners and donors work together so every program is grounded in respect, accountability, and sustainability.

Impact & Transparency

Every donation has a clear path to the field.

We pair donor intent with community priorities through a documented process.

  1. Assessment by field directors and community committees.
  2. Program design with measurable outcomes and budget transparency.
  3. Implementation, monitoring, and storytelling shared in monthly reports.

Transparency

  • Quarterly reports shared online and by request.
  • Documented procurement and volunteer rosters.
  • Responsible spending guided by local oversight groups.
